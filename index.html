Biography

I’m a master student from School of Geodesy and Geomatics, Wuhan University. My research interest includes multi-sensor fusion, Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS), indoor-outdoor seamless positioning, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), and sensor calibration.

I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xingxing Li of GREAT Lab from School of Geodesy and Geomatics, Wuhan University.
